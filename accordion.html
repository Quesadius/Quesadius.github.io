Accordion Solitaire

How to play

All 52 cards are dealt in a single row. A pile may be placed on the pile one position or three positions to its left when the two top cards match in suit or rank. The row closes up after each move.

Tap a card to select it (its legal destinations light up blue), then tap a destination to move. You win by squeezing the whole deck into a single pile; the game ends when no moves remain.
