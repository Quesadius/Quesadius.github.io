Accordion Solitaire

How to play

The row starts empty. Tap the face-down stock at the end of the row to draw the next card into the open spot — you can draw at any time, and make as many moves as you like between draws.

A pile may be placed on the pile one position or three positions to its left when the two top cards match in suit or rank. The row closes up after each move. Tap a card to select it (its legal destinations light up blue), then tap a destination to move.

You win by squeezing the whole deck into a single pile; the game is over when the stock is empty and no moves remain.
